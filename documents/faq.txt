FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

GENERAL QUESTIONS

Q: What is the company's refund policy?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all products. Return the item in original condition with receipt for full refund.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach us via email at support@company.com, phone at 1-800-COMPANY, or live chat on our website.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to over 50 countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT

Q: My device won't turn on, what should I do?
A: First, check if the device is properly plugged in. Try a different power outlet. If the problem persists, contact technical support.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.

Q: The app keeps crashing, how can I fix it?
A: Try closing the app completely and restarting it. Update to the latest version. If issues continue, reinstall the app.

Q: Can I use the service on multiple devices?
A: Yes, you can access your account from up to 5 different devices simultaneously.

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

Q: How do I change my email address?
A: Go to Account Settings > Personal Information > Email Address. Enter your new email and verify it.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
A: Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Go to Account Settings > Subscriptions > Cancel.

Q: How do I update my billing information?
A: Navigate to Account Settings > Billing > Payment Methods to update your card information.

Q: Is my personal information secure?
A: Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal information with third parties.

PRODUCT INFORMATION

Q: What's the difference between Basic and Premium plans?
A: Basic includes core features, Premium adds advanced analytics, priority support, and unlimited storage.

Q: Do you offer volume discounts?
A: Yes, for orders of 10+ units, contact our sales team for custom pricing.

Q: What's included in the warranty?
A: Our standard warranty covers manufacturing defects for 1 year. Extended warranties available for purchase.

Q: Can I upgrade my plan later?
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping: 3-5 business days. Express shipping: 1-2 business days. International: 7-14 business days.

Q: Do you provide tracking numbers?
A: Yes, tracking numbers are sent via email once your order ships.

Q: What if my package arrives damaged?
A: Take photos and contact us within 48 hours. We'll arrange a replacement or refund.

Q: Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
A: Address changes can be made within 2 hours of placing the order by contacting customer service.
