Company Policies Document
1. Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to act with integrity, honesty, and professionalism. Respect towards colleagues, clients, and stakeholders is mandatory. Employees must avoid conflicts of interest, unethical behavior, and misuse of company property.

2. Attendance and Leave Policy

Employees are required to adhere to their designated working hours. Regular attendance and punctuality are essential. Leave requests must be submitted in advance through the official HR system. Unauthorized absences may result in disciplinary action.

3. Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) Policy

The company is an equal opportunity employer. Hiring, promotion, compensation, and training decisions will not be influenced by race, religion, gender, age, disability, or any legally protected category.

4. Workplace Safety Policy

Safety is a shared responsibility. Employees must comply with safety guidelines, report hazards immediately, and use protective equipment where required. Emergency procedures must be followed strictly.

5. Anti-Harassment & Anti-Discrimination Policy

The company strictly prohibits harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind. Any employee experiencing or witnessing inappropriate behavior should report it immediately to HR. Retaliation against whistleblowers is forbidden.

6. Confidentiality & Data Protection Policy

Employees must protect confidential company data, client information, and intellectual property. Sharing sensitive information without authorization is a violation and may lead to termination or legal action. Employees must comply with data protection laws such as GDPR (if applicable).

7. IT & Internet Usage Policy

Company devices, internet, and email systems must be used responsibly and primarily for business purposes. Unauthorized downloads, malicious software installation, or visiting inappropriate websites are prohibited. Personal use should be minimal and not interfere with work.

8. Remote Work Policy

Employees working remotely must maintain the same level of professionalism as in the office. Work hours, communication protocols, and productivity expectations remain the same. Employees are responsible for ensuring a secure and reliable internet connection.

9. Disciplinary Action Policy

Violations of company policies may result in disciplinary actions, including verbal or written warnings, suspension, or termination depending on severity. Serious offenses such as fraud, theft, or harassment may lead to immediate termination.

10. Expense Reimbursement Policy

Employees will be reimbursed for approved business expenses. All claims must be submitted with receipts and within the stipulated timeline. Fraudulent claims may result in disciplinary action.
